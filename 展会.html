Medlab Asia 2025: ASEAN’s Premier IVD and Laboratory Exhibition

From July 16th to 18th, the Malaysia International Trade and Exhibition Centre (MITEC) in Kuala Lumpur hosted Medlab Asia & Asia Health 2025, the largest IVD and laboratory equipment exhibition in the ASEAN region.

Key Highlights of the Exhibition

Global Participation: 500+ enterprises from 54 countries, including industry giants like Roche and Siemens, and Chinese innovators such as Hotgen Biotech.

Audience Reach: 10,000+ professional attendees, with 40% from Thailand, 20% from Malaysia, and 19% from Indonesia.

Market Potential: A strategic platform to tap into the $740 billion ASEAN healthcare market, fostering technology exchange and partnerships.
